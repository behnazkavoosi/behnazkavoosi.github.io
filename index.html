About

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Visualization and Media Technology, with a specialization in Computer Graphics at Linköping University. Throughout my research journey, I am fortunate to have the guidance and support of esteemed supervisors: Jonas Unger, Saghi Hajisharif, and Ehsan Miandji. As a Marie Skłodowska-Curie fellow, I am privileged to be a part of the PRIME project, surrounded by an exceptional group of students and supervisors in the field of computer graphics.

With a background as an electronics engineer focusing on digital electronic systems, I obtained my M.Sc. in Electronics Engineering from Shahid Beheshti University in Iran in 2020. I bring over two years of industry experience as an electronics engineer. Prior to pursuing my master's degree, I completed my B.Sc. in electrical engineering at K.N.Toosi University in Iran. My passion lies in exploring new technologies and continuously expanding my knowledge. As an example, for my master's thesis, I designed a distributed authentication system on Blockchain, specifically tailored for a decentralized barter market.

Currently, I am driven by a deep interest in the world of materials in graphics, aiming to leverage my diverse experience across different fields to push the boundaries of appearance modeling. I am actively searching for an internship opportunity that combines my skills in Computer Graphics, providing a platform for professional development, intriguing experiences, and personal growth.
